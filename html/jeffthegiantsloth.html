Jeff the Giant Sloth

Columbus, Ohio

Travel to the State Capital to see this glorious pre-historic specimen.

"Jeff," is a fossil skeleton of a giant ground sloth (Megalonyx Jeffersoni) and one of the main attractions for the small geological museum that houses him.

Jeff stood about seven feet tall and had a ferocious appetite (for plants thankfully).
